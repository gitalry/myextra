Intro

We sell livestock.
Our clients include large scale animal keepers, breeders, abatoirs, individual, colleges and other institutions.We specialize in animal husbandry, we rear animals and sell them customers from all walks of life.
We breed and sell young animals to farms that do not have the necessary facilities to breed their own young. Our hatcheries are well equiped to hatch chicks for commercial enterprises.
